# Image-to-Image Refinement

## R1 — “Enhance Detail” Suffix
Prompt:
Enhance the image with refined texture, improved sharpness, realistic lighting correction, natural color grading, preserve identity and composition, avoid over-smoothing.

## R2 — Background Swap (Clean Studio)
Prompt:
Keep the subject unchanged. Replace the background with a clean studio gradient backdrop, subtle vignette, softbox lighting, natural shadow under subject.

## R3 — Style Transfer (Cinematic)
Prompt:
Preserve identity and pose. Apply cinematic lighting, volumetric rays, subtle film grain, warm highlight roll-off, shallow depth of field, storytelling atmosphere.

## R4 — Product Mockup Look
Prompt:
Keep product shape and label consistent. Improve material realism, controlled reflections, add soft studio shadow, clean background, commercial packshot.

## R5 — Remove Clutter
Prompt:
Remove distracting background elements. Keep main subject. Simplify scene, clean composition, improve clarity, natural colors.

## R6 — Posterize Layout (Add Text Space)
Prompt:
Recompose into a poster layout with clear negative space for headline text, keep subject prominent, simplify background, modern design.

## R7 — Face Consistency Fix
Prompt:
Maintain the same facial identity as the reference. Improve facial symmetry naturally, keep skin texture realistic (pores visible), fix eye catchlights, avoid plastic skin.

## R8 — Upscale Feeling (Pseudo 4K)
Prompt:
Increase perceived resolution: add micro texture details, sharpen edges naturally, enhance lighting depth, keep noise minimal, avoid artifacts.
